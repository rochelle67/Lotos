Who Are We?

We are makers of scents and we use olfactory compositions to tell compelling stories. Using quality natural ingredients we craft deep aromatic landscapes that allow people to indulge in their own senses. We reject trends and the pull of the industrial. Our products are a celebration of plants, the air, and our place in nature. We are a small Alberta based company but are growing as more people hear about our products.
